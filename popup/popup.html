Find Ward Sex Offenders
  1. Log into the Ward Directory.
  2. Go to the National Sex Offender Search Page and enter either a 'Zipcode' or a 'Radius Search'.
  3. After the results page loads, click the "Show Possible Ward Members" button.
All matches are best-guesses based on zipcode and matching names between nspow.gov and the ward directory. You are responsible for verifying the identity of matches. This is not guaranteed to find all sex offenders in a ward. By using this extension you agree to use the matches found for information only.
